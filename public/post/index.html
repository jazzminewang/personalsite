[Normative] Progress Studies - a literature review

Introduction Progress, as defined by Tyler Cowen and Patrick Collison, as “the combination of economic, technological, scientific, cultural, and organizational advancement that has transformed our lives and raised standards of living over the past couple of centuries”1. Progress is ‘pre-paradigmatic’; for researchers to conduct ‘normal research’, a research agenda must be set. _This is a first attempt at thinking about the relevant literature to inherit from in thinking about scientific and technological progress.
